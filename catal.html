01

republish a text

this project is a compiled list of every instance in which the character sybil vane is described and pasted into various craigslist listings in the missed connection's section. the object was to see if any individuals would project themselves onto these descriptions of a fictional character and respond to the ads, which were inconspicuously labeled to fit the contemporary time.
02

twenty variations

wilfred owen's poem, dulce et decorum est, is reformatted twenty different times. every five versions operates under certain constraints, the simplest and strictest restraints at versions one through five, the most unregulated in versions fifteen to twenty.
03

this is an event invitation for a fictional brand, the convoluted construction of which explores themes of capitalism, commodification, and cooptation.
04

screenshot photography

this is a series of fifteen screenshots taken over the course of sixteen weeks. some explore narrative construction through the combination of different assets (i.e. reading a sentence across several tab titles), while others are minimalist stagings of mac workspaces. others also invoke the creation of collages and juxtaposing certain text and images or evaluating a context.
